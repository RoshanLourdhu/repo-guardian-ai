\# Summary Generator



\## Purpose

Provide an overall health assessment of the repository.



\## Instructions

\- Combine results from bug, security, and improvement analysis

\- Count total issues and categorize them

\- Generate a trust score based on severity and frequency

\- Provide a final recommendation (Safe / Moderate / Risky)



\## Output

Return a structured summary including:

\- Total issues

\- Key findings

\- Trust score

\- Final recommendation
